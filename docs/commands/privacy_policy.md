# `/privacy_policy` - Privacy Policy

**Usage:** `/privacy_policy`

## Description
Displays WompBot's complete privacy policy explaining data collection, processing, and your rights under GDPR.

## What You'll Learn
- **Legal Basis**: Why and how WompBot collects data (Legitimate Interest - GDPR Art. 6.1.f)
- **Data Collected**: What information is stored about you
- **Data Usage**: How your data is used to provide features
- **Third-Party Sharing**: Which external services receive data (OpenRouter, Tavily, iRacing)
- **Your Rights**: How to access, export, delete, or opt out of data collection
- **Retention Periods**: How long data is stored
- **Security Measures**: How your data is protected

## Default Data Processing
WompBot operates under an **opt-out model**:
- All users are opted-IN by default
- Data collection enables personalized responses and full feature access
- You can opt out anytime with `/wompbot_optout`

## Privacy Commands
- `/wompbot_optout` - Stop data collection
- `/download_my_data` - Export all your data (GDPR Art. 15)
- `/delete_my_data` - Request deletion (GDPR Art. 17)
- `/my_privacy_status` - Check your current status

## Related Commands
- `/tos` - Terms of Service
- `/my_privacy_status` - Check your privacy status
- `/privacy_support` - Get help with privacy questions

## Compliance
- GDPR-compliant (EU General Data Protection Regulation)
- Based on Legitimate Interest with opt-out rights
- Full data subject rights implemented
- Transparent about data processing
